Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync with iCloud?

Five. automatically syncs with iCloud when you're signed in to your Apple ID. No additional setup required.

Why does Five. need calendar access?

Calendar access allows Five. to display your events alongside tasks for better schedule management.

How do I set reminders?

Long press on any task and select "Add Reminder" to set a notification time.

Privacy

Five. respects your privacy. We only collect necessary data for app functionality:

  • Calendar data (with permission)
  • Location for weather (with permission)
  • Tasks stored in iCloud

No personal data is shared with third parties.
