Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Five. ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Calendar Data

With your permission, we access your calendar to display events alongside your tasks. This data is only stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Location Data

When authorized, we collect your location data to provide weather information. This data is not stored and is only used to fetch weather information from Apple's WeatherKit.

Task Data

Your tasks and preferences are stored locally on your device and synced with your personal iCloud account if enabled.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Display calendar events in the app
  • Provide local weather information
  • Sync your tasks across devices via iCloud
  • Send local notifications for task reminders

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not maintain any servers or databases to store your information.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's WeatherKit for weather data and iCloud for data synchronization. These services are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Control app permissions through your device settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage iCloud sync settings in your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
